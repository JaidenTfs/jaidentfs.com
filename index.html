About JaidenTfs

"Hey, It's me JaidenTfs, a composer with ambitious dreams in the music industry. I want to produce scores for games, film, and other interactive media."

Jaiden is a 17-year-old who loves everything music.
If you would like to contact him, the best way is through email or Instagram dms.

One of my recent experiences was a 48-hour game jam, where I composed the music for a game called "Little Guy, Big Sword." The game follows a character thrown into an underground arena, surviving waves of enemies before facing a final boss—a winged creature with explosive attacks. The music was designed to heighten the action, quiet moments, and the emotional highs and lows of victory and defeat.

I’ve also contributed to the ARG project "Dear Clarent," working with Imbernet Entertainment, an indie studio focused on creating realistic fiction inspired by historical events and figures. In this project, I worked with the founder and another composer to create the "Dear Clarent Songbook," a collection of songs from the game's world thatt tell emotional stories through music. Along with composing original tracks, I’ve helped plan features for the project.

Most of the games I've scored are playable for free on my itch.io page -- if you try one out, I'd love to hear what you thought in the comments there.
